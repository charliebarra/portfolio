Featured Case Study

Meteor Mayhem

An original multiplayer strategy card game about mining resources, upgrading ships, managing risk, and responding to hazards.

View GitHub RepositoryWatch Video
RoleSole Designer & Creator
Year2025
StatusPlayable Prototype
FocusBalance + Decisions

Context

An independent honors project.

I developed Meteor Mayhem through Life Design Lab as an independent honors project. The program encouraged me to work through a design process: identify a question, research possibilities, build a prototype, test it with real people, and revise based on what I learned. I used those ideas as a guide, but the project changed as I tested it.

Design Challenge

Can luck matter without deciding who wins?

I wanted the game to feel strategic and unpredictable. Players needed real choices about when to take risks, upgrade, defend, or interfere with another player. I used probability reasoning and card-frequency analysis to adjust how often different effects appeared, but playtesting determined whether the game actually felt fair.

Process

01Research

Studied resource systems, upgrades, and player interaction.

Reflection

Players always teach the designer something.

The biggest lesson was that players interact with a system differently than the designer predicts. Watching them helped me improve the game more than repeatedly explaining what I intended.
