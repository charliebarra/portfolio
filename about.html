How I work

Build, test, learn, revise.

Start with curiosity

I usually begin by asking what makes a mechanic or interaction interesting.

Make something playable

I learn more from a rough prototype than from keeping an idea perfect in my head.

Watch what breaks

Testing shows where the system is confusing, too random, too powerful, or not fun.

Use feedback

Other players notice things I miss. Their choices help me understand the system better.

Think about balance

I like making games feel strategic without making them predictable.

Keep improving

The best version usually comes after several versions that did not work.
