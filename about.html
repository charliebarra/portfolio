About

I like figuring out why systems work.

Hi, I’m Charlie. I’m interested in game design, programming, cybersecurity, and interactive systems — especially projects where small design choices can change how people think, play, and solve problems.

Game design • strategy • systems

From player to designer.

My interest in game design grew out of years of competitive Pokémon. Over time, I became just as interested in why games worked as I was in winning them. That curiosity led me to Roblox, programming, cybersecurity, and eventually designing Meteor Mayhem, my own strategy card game.

How I work

Build, test, learn, revise.

Start with curiosity

I usually begin by asking what makes a mechanic, system, or interaction interesting.

Make something playable

I learn more from a rough prototype than from keeping an idea perfect in my head.

Watch what breaks

Testing shows where the system is confusing, too random, too powerful, or just not fun.

Use feedback

Other players notice things I miss. Their choices help me understand the system better.

Think about balance

I like the challenge of making games feel strategic without making them predictable.

Keep improving

The best version of a project usually comes after several versions that did not work.
