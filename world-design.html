Interactive World Design

From player to creator.

Roblox and Minecraft made me realize that I did not only want to play in other people’s worlds. I wanted to build and change my own. These were personal projects where I built in Roblox Studio, experimented with Lua, and usually changed the idea halfway through.

Visual Direction

Collecting ideas before building.

I assembled these boards as a kid using images I found online. I did not draw the artwork. I used the boards to collect ideas for characters, architecture, materials, and sci-fi environments before I started building.

Design Studies

Three small experiments.

Study 01

Environment Concept

I wanted to see how a world could tell the player what kind of game it was before anything really happened. I focused on the environment, the mood, and what the space seemed to promise.

Player Space Prototype

Systems / Interaction Test
