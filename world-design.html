Interactive World Design

From player to creator.

Roblox and Minecraft made me realize that I did not only want to play in other people’s worlds. I wanted to build and change my own. These were personal explorations using Roblox Studio building and Lua scripting.

Visual Direction

Collecting ideas before building.

I assembled these boards as a kid using images I found through online searches. I did not draw the artwork. I used it to collect references for character tone, architecture, materials, and sci-fi environments.

Design Studies

Three small experiments.

Study 01

Environment Concept

I was experimenting with how a world feels before the player really knows what to do. I focused on the environment, the mood, and what kind of game the space seemed to promise.

Study 02

Player Space Prototype

This one was more about moving around the space. I changed the layout and scale to see how they affected where the player went and what they noticed first.

Systems / Interaction Test
