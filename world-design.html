Interactive World Design

From player to creator.

Roblox introduced me to the idea that players could also become creators. These studies helped me experiment with atmosphere, navigation, interaction, and how small design choices change the way a player experiences a space.

Visual Direction

Each study focused on a different design question: atmosphere, navigation, or interaction.
