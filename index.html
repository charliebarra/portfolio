North Star

I’m most interested in the moment when playing a game turns into wondering how it works.

My projects include competitive Pokémon, an original strategy card game, Roblox design studies, programming, cybersecurity, and probability simulation. They look different, but they usually begin the same way: I notice something, wonder why it happens, and try building something to test it.

My Story

Every designer starts as a player.

I started with games like Super Mario Bros., Disney Infinity, Mario Kart, Animal Crossing, and Exploding Kittens. Later, Roblox and Minecraft showed me that players could build their own worlds too. Competitive Pokémon made me curious about probability and preparation, and eventually I started making games and programming projects of my own.
