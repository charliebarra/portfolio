My story

Every designer starts as a player.

Age 3-8

From an early age, games like New Super Mario Bros. 2 introduced me to level progression, timing, exploration, and player-focused design. These experiences sparked my initial interest in how games create challenge, reward, and engagement.

Age 7-10

Started seeing games, like Roblox and Minecraft, as spaces where players could also become creators..

Age 9

Competed at my first major Pokémon championship and learned that preparation matters.

Age 10

Placed Top 64 at Regionals and qualified for Worlds before the event was cancelled.

Age 15

Designed a strategy card game, Meteor Mayhem, from rough ideas to a playable prototype.

Age 15-17

Built practical technical skills during Cybersecurity courses and learned how complex systems fail and get fixed.

Next

Building Python games, simulation tools, and interactive learning systems.
