North Star

I’m most interested in the space between playing a game and understanding why it works.

My projects connect competitive Pokémon, an original strategy card game, Roblox design studies, programming, cybersecurity, and probability simulation. They look different on the surface, but they all come back to the same question: what makes a game or experience interesting enough to keep thinking about?

My Story

Every designer starts as a player.

I started with games like Super Mario Bros., Disney Infinity, Mario Kart, Animal Crossing, and Exploding Kittens. Later, Roblox and Minecraft showed me that players could become creators too. Competitive Pokémon made me curious about probability and preparation, and eventually I began designing games and programming projects of my own.
