Featured Project

Meteor Mayhem

Meteor Mayhem is an original multiplayer strategy card game about mining resources from a meteor, upgrading ships, managing risk, and disrupting opponents through event cards and environmental hazards.

RoleLead Designer & Creator
Year2025
StatusPlayable Prototype
FocusBalance + Decision-Making

Design Challenge

Can luck matter without deciding who wins?

I wanted Meteor Mayhem to feel strategic and unpredictable at the same time. Players needed to make meaningful decisions about timing, risk, upgrades, defense, and interaction. The challenge was keeping uncertainty exciting without making the outcome feel random.

01Research

Studied games with resource systems, upgrades, and player interaction.

02Ideation

Brainstormed mechanics, card types, disasters, and win conditions.

03Prototype

Built a playable physical version with cards and components.

04Testing

Watched real players interact with the system in unexpected ways.

05Iteration

Refined balance, pacing, and card effects based on feedback.

Process Evidence

The messy part matters.

A lot of the real design happened before the final prototype existed. The planning sheets, notes, and card revisions show how the project changed as I tested ideas and simplified mechanics.

Card System

Readable cards, clear categories, fast decisions.

I used consistent layouts and clear card categories so players could understand effects quickly. The goal was to keep players focused on strategy instead of constantly stopping to decode the rules.
